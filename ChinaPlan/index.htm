Notice of the Beijing Municipal People's Government of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee on Printing and Distributing the Beijing Municipal Action Plan for Further Optimizing the Business Environment (2018-2020)

发布日期： 2018-11-23 Source: Release date: 2018-11-23

Each district committee, district government, municipal party committee, municipal government ministries and commissions bureau, various head offices, various people's organizations, colleges and universities:

The "Beijing Further Optimization of the Business Environment Action Plan (2018-2020)" will be printed and distributed to you. Please implement it carefully in light of the actual situation.

CPC Beijing Municipal Committee

Beijing Municipal People's Government

July 18, 2018

Beijing further optimizes the business environment action plan (2018-2020)


First, the overall requirements

(1) Guiding ideology

Fully implement the spirit of the 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, take Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics as the guide, adhere to the new development concept, firmly grasp the strategic positioning of the capital city, give full play to the capital's scientific and technological innovation and talent advantages, and take the rule of law and internationalization. Facilitating and facilitating, with the practical enhancement of the sense of gains from enterprises and the masses, focusing on accelerating service-oriented government and smart government construction, taking innovation and development and opening up of service industry as a breakthrough, consolidating and enhancing existing advantages and comprehensively reinforcing Short-term weaknesses, solidly promote Beijing efficiency, Beijing service, Beijing standards and Beijing integrity “four major demonstration projects of business environment”, and strive to build a departmental life cycle service system with departmental synergy, urban linkage, market support and joint promotion. Make Beijing a world-class business environment and promote the high-quality development of the capital.

(2) Working principle

Adhere to the people as the center. The sense of gains from business and the masses is the sole criterion for measuring the effectiveness of work. In the reform, I will take the initiative to change my thinking and change “What should I do” as “What should I do for enterprises and the masses?”, paying attention to enterprise experience, re-approval approval process, and innovation support policies, striving to create the least approval, the best process, and the highest efficiency. The business environment is the best, and the business has the strongest business environment.

Adhere to reform and innovation. Dare to dare to try, dare to be the first, dare to open the knife to the stubborn disease, the courage to break through the benefits of solidification. Accelerate the construction and improvement of the system and mechanism that is conducive to the vitality of market participants, create a good environment for enterprises to participate in the development of the capital, and continuously create and accumulate experience for optimizing the business environment in China.

Adhere to the problem-oriented. Targeting the pain points, difficulties, and blocking problems involved in the whole life cycle of establishment, construction, operation, and development, targeting, tackling, precision, and comprehensive measures. Adhere to the market-led and government-led "two-handedness", not only to speed up the construction of service-oriented government, but also to improve the professionalization and internationalization level of intermediaries, mobilize the whole society, and promote the formation of a new pattern of optimizing the business environment in Beijing.

Adhere to the first-class benchmark. Actively benchmarking international advanced concepts, leading standards and best practices, further broaden market access and create a more attractive investment and trading environment. Further improve the internationalization level of relevant systems and rules, continue to enhance international competitiveness and open development capabilities, and strive to be the "leaders" of the new open economy system.

(3) Working framework

With the "six unified" intelligent government service system as the technical support, strengthen the construction of the rule of law government and service-oriented government, accelerate the promotion of big data action plan, and strive to build Beijing efficiency, Beijing service, Beijing standard and Beijing integrity "business environment four Great demonstration project, strive to form brand influence and drive in the country.

"Six unified" intelligent government service system. Through the construction of the city's unified credit information platform, the city's unified online and offline government service hall, the city's unified enterprise services and major project platforms, the city's unified corporate legal person service card, the city's unified investment project code and the city's unified government services Standards and regulations, the implementation of the examination and approval service "immediately, online, close to the office, once to do", to promote the flat, simplified and intelligent examination and approval services, to achieve government information and data sharing, and strengthen the legal supervision and credit supervision after the event, Comprehensively optimize and enhance the experience of enterprises and the masses.

“Beijing Efficiency” demonstration project. Strengthen the top-level design of smart government affairs, carry out the reform of “distribution service” in depth, focus on the administrative examination and approval items of technical standards and regulations, and take the principle of one item and one approval at a time, further cancel the examination and approval items, re-create the approval process, and reduce the time limit for approval. To create a unified service model for “wide-entry, fast-running, strict management, convenient and open” in the city, and to serve the enterprises and the masses with the shortest time, the fastest speed and the most effective supervision. First-class "Beijing efficiency."

"Beijing Service" demonstration project. Firmly establish the awareness of service enterprises, and carry out the optimization and improvement of the service experience throughout the reform, systematically solve the "last mile" and work style problems that the enterprise reflects prominently, and guide the cadres of the city to serve the "shop 2" of the service enterprises in good faith, and do "required, "Nothing to do without interference", honesty and mutual trust with entrepreneurs, and fully demonstrate the "Beijing service" of "pro-" and "clear" new political-business relations.

"Beijing Standard" demonstration project. From the height of promoting the modernization of the governance capacity of the super-large urban governance system, in accordance with the principles of fairness, justice, openness and transparency, on the basis of actively exploring and extensively soliciting opinions, we will establish an industry that meets the strategic positioning of the capital city and reflects the high-quality development requirements of the new era. Development standards, urban planning standards, technical service standards, and market supervision standards are encouraged to encourage enterprises to actively create international and domestic advanced standards, enhance the authority, seriousness, and operability of standards, and fully display the "Beijing Standards" that can be replicated and promoted.

"Beijing Integrity" demonstration project. Efforts will be made to build a market supervision mechanism with corporate credit as the core, accelerate the collection and sharing of government and corporate credit information, openly and openly, explore the implementation of the “personal integrity” project, and promote credit information in market access, public services, travel, It is widely used in fields such as entrepreneurship and job hunting. Promote the pilot demonstration of the “Xinyi+” project, and provide convenience measures such as “acceptance and acceptance” and “green channel” for the trustworthy. We will improve the blacklist system and form a pattern of dishonesty and punishment that is “one place is untrustworthy, limited everywhere, and difficult to move”, so that those who violate the law and lose the trust will pay a heavy price. Guide enterprises and individuals to establish their business with integrity, and fully demonstrate the "Beijing Integrity" that leads the country.

(4) Work objectives

By the end of 2018, the business environment of the city has been greatly improved, and the “six unified” smart government service system construction framework has been completed. More than 80% of municipal government service affairs have achieved “one network management”, starting enterprises, handling construction permits, and cross- The reforms in border trade, access to electricity, and real estate registration have made breakthroughs. The “Four Demonstration Projects for Business Environment” has started construction, and the business environment ranks among the top in the country.

By the end of 2019, the level of legalization, internationalization, and facilitation of the business environment in this city has been significantly improved. The “six unified” intelligent government service system has taken initial shape, and more than 90% of the municipal and district government affairs services have achieved “One Netcom”. The service level of starting a business, handling construction permits, cross-border trade, obtaining electricity, and registering real estate has been significantly improved. The “Four Demonstration Projects for Business Environment” has been accelerated, and the business environment has achieved national leadership.

By the end of 2020, the level of legalization, internationalization, and facilitation of the business environment in this city has been significantly improved. The “six unified” smart government service system has been fully formed, and 100% of the municipal and district government affairs services have achieved “one network service”. "The services of starting enterprises, handling construction permits, cross-border trade, obtaining electricity, and registering real estates have been significantly improved. The "Four Demonstration Projects for Business Environment" has a wide impact and will further play a leading role in the country.

Second, the main task

(1) Creating a more convenient government environment

1. Significantly improve the efficiency of investment approval services. (1) Deepen the pilot of investment approval reform for public service construction projects, and promote the pilot experience into a unified national investment project approval management system. (2) Substantially compressing the preliminary approval items and links of investment projects. We will improve the scientific compilation mechanism of “multi-level integration” of controlled detailed planning, and explore the evaluation of “regional planning and evaluation” alternative projects one by one. In principle, one item is implemented at most once. A department can formulate technical standards and regulations, implement an enterprise project commitment system, gradually abolish administrative examination and approval, and comprehensively improve the efficiency of construction permit approval. (3) Optimize and upgrade the online approval and supervision platform for Beijing investment projects, improve the functions of up and down, vertical and horizontal connectivity, introduce management measures, integrate the “multi-regulation” collaborative platform, construction drawing joint review platform, joint acceptance platform, and real estate registration system. Achieve investment project approval "one network." (4) Fully implement the unified investment project code in the joint investigation, joint review, joint mapping, joint acceptance, real estate registration and other links to achieve the full life cycle of investment projects. (5) Accelerate the construction of an online service service platform in the field of real estate registration, strengthen the linkage of housing transactions, tax payment, real estate registration system and data sharing, and realize the transformation of the examination and approval service from “physical aggregation” to “chemical synthesis”, and strive to reduce the real estate registration time limit to 1 to 4 days. Integrate geographic information systems and housing registration systems to achieve real estate registration and cadastral surveying and mapping using real estate numbers. Enhance the transparency of real estate registration and related fields, formulate the city's real estate information inquiry rules, and realize the public inquiry of real estate real estate conditions, restrictions on disposition information and land ownership dispute cases. More types of property rights such as the promotion of forest rights and the right to use homesteads will be included in the unified registration. (Responsible unit: Municipal Government Reform Office, Municipal Planning State Land Committee, Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Housing and Urban Construction Committee, Municipal Service Office, Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau, Municipal High Court, Municipal Agricultural Committee)

2. Deepen the reform of the commercial system. (1) Improve the efficiency of starting a business. By the end of 2018, the start-up time of enterprises will be reduced to less than 3 days; by the end of 2020, efforts will be reduced to less than 2 days. (2) Improve the function of “e-window” system, carry out online invoices, “five insurances and one gold” registration, etc., further merge business affairs, and realize bank reservations. (3) Implement a unified corporate legal person service card, cancel the corporate service key of each department, and let the corporate legal person service card become the only pass for the enterprise to do business. (4) Continue to deepen the reform of “separation of licenses” and “multi-integration of certificates”, and further integrate various licenses belonging to information collection, record publicity and management for inspection after the reform of “testimony separation” to the business license, and realize “multi-certification” One, one photo, one yard." (5) Explore the “one-touch multiple address” of some life service enterprises in the city six districts and the Beijing city sub-center. (6) Do a better job of simplifying the cancellation of registration and registration, explore and optimize the enterprise cancellation procedures, and pilot and promote the electronic cancellation of enterprise cancellation. (7) Accelerate the mutual recognition and exchange of electronic business licenses, promote the use of electronic seals, and expand the use of electronic invoices. (Responsible units: Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce, Municipal Government Reform Office, People's Bank Business Management Department, Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau, Municipal Public Security Bureau, Municipal Housing Provident Fund Management Center, Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, Municipal Economic Information Commission, City Six District and Tongzhou District committee and district government)

3. Advance the whole municipal service "one window, one network". (1) Establish an online and offline government service hall of the city's unified Unicom, promote coordinated operations, integrated services and information sharing between urban areas and departments, and promote the transformation of enterprises and people from "finding departments" to "finding the government." (2) Integrate the service windows of the divisions into a comprehensive window, and fully implement the work mode of “integrated reception at the front desk, approval of background classification, and unification of windows” to realize “one window handling” in the city. Establish a high-quality, professional and comprehensive window staff team, and actively explore the market service level through the market-based means and assessment mechanism. (3) Accelerate the implementation of the relevant requirements for the construction of the “one network” of the municipal service, clean up and integrate the decentralized and independent government service information system, and all access the unified government service network and municipal government cloud to realize the “one network office” of the city. . Build a network security protection system to achieve reliable exchange and security sharing of government information across departments, regions, and levels, and open to the society according to laws and regulations. (4) Optimize the examination and approval department's approval department setting, and promote the conditional approval department to integrate the examination and approval function and set up the examination and approval department to enter the government service center. Actively strive to carry out pilot reforms of relatively centralized administrative licensing rights in the Beijing Economic and Technological Development Zone, explore the establishment of an administrative examination and approval authority, and implement “one seal management approval”. (5) Clean up the administrative examination and approval intermediary service matters, standardize the intermediary service matters for management retention, rely on the government service network to develop and construct an intermediary service online trading platform, and the intermediary service organization “zero threshold, zero limit” to settle in, realize online display, online bidding, online Winning the bid, online evaluation, and exploring the implementation of the "full-time help system." (Responsible unit: Municipal Government Reform Office, Municipal Affairs Service Office, Municipal Economic Informationization Committee, Beijing Economic and Technological Development Zone Administrative Committee, and District Committees and Districts Government)

4. Standardize government service standards. (1) In accordance with the requirements of matching powers and responsibilities, having the right to be responsible, and failing to pursue the law, we will promote the list of powers and the list of responsibilities, “two-in-one integration”, and achieve “one table and two orders”. Establish and improve a long-term mechanism for the work of clearing certificates, formulate a catalogue of the third batch of cancellation certificates in this Municipality, and compile a catalogue of temporary retention certificates for this Municipality. Dynamically adjust the list of professional qualification licenses. (2) Formulate the city's unified government service service standard standards, service guides, practical manuals and application materials specifications, comprehensively eliminate the vague and stipulated clauses in the examination and approval service, and construct and improve the formal and easy-to-understand examination and approval service procedures. Figure, the same conditions in the city to achieve the same conditions without differences. (3) In accordance with the principle of “convenience for the benefit of the people and the best of both worlds”, we will further promote the integration of the government service hotline and gradually realize the “No. 1 Link” of the city. (4) Establish a “dual platform” working mechanism for enterprise services and major projects, improve the city's enterprise development and project landing information database, and build a service lifecycle development service chain that integrates policy support, factor allocation, coordinated scheduling, and precise services. Innovative attracting talents to attract intelligence and service enterprises, efficiently solve key problems that restrict enterprise development and project landing, and provide more intimate services for enterprises in Beijing development and project landing. (Responsible unit: Municipal Government Reform Office, Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, Municipal Affairs Service Office, Municipal Letters and Visits Office, Municipal Development and Reform Commission)

(2) Creating a more open investment and trade environment

5. Further relax market access. (1) Actively strive to launch a new round of comprehensive pilot projects for expanding and opening up the service industry in Beijing, formulate a pilot work plan, accelerate the implementation of the opening-up policy of the national financial industry, promote the expansion of the service industry at a higher level, and further improve the quality and level of foreign investment utilization. . (2) Foreign investment in the full implementation of the pre-entry national treatment plus negative list management system, adhere to the "four centers" strategic positioning to expand the opening up, and continue to reduce the negative list of services. Based on the construction of the cultural center, we will focus on promoting the opening of the cultural and entertainment industry. Focus on the construction of international exchange centers, and focus on promoting the international development of business services and air transport. Focusing on the construction of science and technology innovation centers, we will focus on promoting the innovation elements in the fields of finance and information and the high-end links of the value chain to further expand and open up. (Responsible units: Municipal Commercial Committee, Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Cultural Bureau, Municipal Press and Publication Bureau of Radio and Television, Civil Affairs Bureau, Municipal Justice Bureau, Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, Municipal Tourism Commission, Municipal Health and Family Planning Commission, Municipal Financial Bureau, Municipality Economic Information Commission, Municipal Science and Technology Commission, Municipal Quality Supervision Bureau, Municipal Intellectual Property Office and other relevant departments)

6. Effectively expand private investment. (1) Comprehensively sort out and adjust the “revolving door” and “spring door” policies that cancel the entry of non-basic public services and affordable housing, which hinder the entry of private capital, and refine the task list for solving the “last mile” problem. (2) In accordance with the characteristics of private investment, formulate appropriate entry and encouragement policies in land supply, regional development, and major projects to effectively enhance the market competitiveness of private capital. (3) Fully mobilize the enthusiasm of private enterprises, support the development of a mixed-ownership economy, and actively promote the reform of the mixed ownership system of state-owned enterprises in the business sector. (4) In accordance with the work mechanism of “finding the introduction of a batch, planning a batch of reserves, integrating a batch of work, sorting out and promoting a batch, and speeding up the promotion of a batch”, a series of fields oriented to private capital, industry-oriented, and favorable Demonstration project for transformation and upgrading. (Responsible unit: Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Planning and Land Commission, Municipal Housing and Urban Construction Committee, Municipal Major Project Office, Municipal Investment Promotion Bureau, Municipal Education Commission, Municipal Medical Reform Office, Municipal Health and Family Planning Commission, Civil Affairs Bureau, Municipal State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission)

7. Increase the intensity of bidding reform. (1) Implement relevant policy documents for national bidding and tendering, substantially narrow the scope of the necessary bidding projects, and improve the standardization standards for the necessary bidding projects. In principle, social investment projects shall be decided by the enterprises themselves to bid and tender, and release investment vitality. (2) Integrate and establish a unified and standardized public resource transaction service platform for the whole city, comprehensively sort out and standardize the service guidelines and business processes of industry, field and regional public resource transaction systems. (3) Further reduce the cost of bidding and bidding transactions, and the public resource trading places established by relevant government departments and affiliated institutions shall be exempt from the bidding and tendering service fee for the construction projects of the entering transaction. (4) Fully implement electronic bidding and tendering, expand the scope of application of electronic trading platforms, and effectively reduce the transaction costs of enterprises. (Responsible unit: Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Planning and Land Commission, Municipal Housing and Urban Construction Committee, District Governments)

8. Continue to improve the level of trade facilitation. (1) Formulate and implement the three-year action plan for promoting the “One Belt and One Road” construction in this Municipality, give full play to the role of “four platforms” for foreign exchanges, humanities exchanges, scientific and technological support, and service support, and support competent and qualified enterprises to actively and steadily carry out overseas operations. Investment activities. (2) Deepen the reform of customs clearance integration, speed up the deep integration of customs inspection, unify declaration documents, unify the operation system, unify risk judgments, issue unified instructions, and unify on-site law enforcement. (3) Upgrade the “single window” of Beijing international trade, expand the scope of business processing to areas such as bonded and service trade, and achieve full coverage of major business declarations by 2020. Promote the whole process of covering the ship's arrival, port operations, cargo clearance and other aspects of paperless, and realize the electronic management of certificate certification in the trade field. Completely cancel the customs clearance form for inbound goods and customs clearance forms for outbound goods, simplifying customs clearance procedures. Improve the standard of sampling inspection, and greatly reduce the inspection rate for low-risk goods and high-credit enterprises. The average inspection rate of exports for the whole year is controlled within 2%. (4) Gradually expand the scope of application of “collecting taxation, self-reporting and self-payment”, and innovating the management mode of “advance declaration, inspection and inspection, and release of documents”. (5) Clean up and regulate the charges at the port of the Capital Airport, further increase the proportion of self-service customs clearance personnel, and create a convenient and efficient customs clearance environment for inbound and outbound passengers. Establish an imported drug testing center in the Tianzhu Comprehensive Bonded Zone to optimize the customs clearance services for imported drugs. (6) Improve the Beijing-Tianjin joint working mechanism, coordinate and promote Tianjin Port to carry out special actions to reduce costs, improve the “one-stop sunshine price” list, comprehensively optimize port operations and customs clearance business processes, and strive to build a national smart port. Demonstration projects, various services have reached the national leading level. (Responsible units: Municipal Commercial Committee, Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Food and Drug Administration, Beijing Customs, Municipal Government Port Office, Beijing Border Inspection Station, Capital Airport Group Corporation, Tianzhu Comprehensive Insurance Zone Management Committee)

(3) Creating a more superior environment for innovation and entrepreneurship

9. Comprehensively enhance the carrying capacity and competitiveness of science and technology innovation. (1) Improve the support policy for high-tech development. We will closely follow the guidance of “10+3” for the development of high-tech industries, study and formulate specific supporting measures, and strive to open up the “last mile” of the policy, and give full play to the benefits of policy combination. (2) Accelerate the construction of a smart city in the “Three Cities and One District”. Adhere to the concept of internationalization and open development, build a network service platform for the National Science and Technology Innovation Center, increase the promotion and application of information technology such as the Internet of Things and big data, and infiltrate “wisdom” into every detail of the construction and operation of the park, giving priority to promoting wisdom. Traffic, smart government, smart community, smart energy pilot, the overall improvement of the "three cities and one district" wisdom development level. (3) Expanding the space for innovation and entrepreneurship. Accelerate the construction of innovative and entrepreneurial carriers such as the creation of space, promote the development of international top innovation and entrepreneurial service institutions and incubators, and build a world-class technology incubator service chain. In the city, a number of urban innovation complexes will be built. According to the individualized needs of innovative entrepreneurs, we will create an innovative ecological environment that integrates innovative services, business, living and living facilities. (4) Deepen the innovation of scientific and technological achievements transformation system. Completion of the legislation on the promotion of the transformation of scientific and technological achievements in Beijing. We will build a coordinated and service platform for the transformation of scientific and technological achievements, and accelerate the transformation of scientific and technological achievements. Give play to the role of Beijing Science and Technology Innovation Fund, establish and improve the guidance and operation mechanism of various innovative industry funds, and focus on supporting high-end “hard technology” innovations such as original innovation, transformation of results and cultivation of high-tech industries. Increase the incentives for scientific research personnel to transform the equity of scientific and technological achievements, and comprehensively improve the conversion rate of scientific and technological achievements. We will improve Zhongguancun's first (set) major technical equipment demonstration application support policy, and actively promote the demonstration and application of new technologies and new products. (Responsible unit: Municipal People's Congress Standing Committee Legal Affairs Office, Municipal Science and Technology Commission, Municipal Economic Information Commission, Municipal Planning Land Commission, Municipal Development and Reform Commission, Municipal Commercial Commission, Municipal Financial Bureau, Municipal Communications Commission, Municipal Education Commission, Municipal Health and Family Planning Commission Municipal Finance Bureau, Municipal Cultural Assets Office, Municipal Government Legal Affairs Office, Zhongguancun Management Committee, Beijing Economic and Technological Development Zone Administrative Committee, and District Committees and Districts and Governments)

10. Provide better services for innovative talents. (1) Strengthen the protection of imported talents. Unblock the "green channel" for the introduction of talents, implement the foreign-invested entry and exit facilitation policy, optimize the service for returnees from overseas study, and further innovate policies to support the "three cities and one district" and the Beijing city sub-center to introduce outstanding talents. (2) Strengthen the housing guarantee for talents. In accordance with the principle of “rental purchase and purchase, rent-based, production-city integration, and occupational residence balance”, the public rental housing is mainly rented, and the joint ownership housing is supplemented. In the area of ​​employment and entrepreneurial talents, the housing is raised to solve the talents. Residential needs. (3) Optimize the talent life service package. Accelerate the allocation of quality education and medical resources in the talented areas and key industrial functional areas such as “Three Cities and One District”, rationally arrange a number of high-end educational institutions and international medical institutions that are in line with international standards, and open up “green channels” to support schools for children of foreign nationals. Landing, open the international medical insurance landing channel. (4) Inspire all kinds of talents. The classification promotes the reform of the talent evaluation mechanism and explores the right to review titles in enterprises and research institutes. Accelerate the construction of the pilot zone for the talent management reform of Zhongguancun. (5) Deeply promote the construction of the international talent community in the capital. Deepen the pilot project of Chaoyang Wangjing, Zhongguancun Street, Future Science City, and New Shougang High-end Industrial Comprehensive Service Area for international talent community construction, and expand the pilot scope to Beijing Economic and Technological Development Zone, Tongzhou District, Shunyi District and Huairou District in time to create suitable international high-end talents.
