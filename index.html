Nico Zevallos is an artist and roboticist working out of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. His research concerns affect and automation, with an emphasis on simulation and robotics. He works across areas in philosophy, religion, science and technology, with interests in determinism, computer graphics, cinema and rats. He doesn't think you will ever download your mind and doesn't think universal income will solve the problems of capitalism.
