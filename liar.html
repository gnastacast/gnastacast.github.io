Liar

I am a parody of my need to create. I am Matthew Barney, Jackson Pollock, Narcissus and Adonis. I strut for the camera, for an audience, nervously flicking smutz from an envious womb, worrying at a slab of time that has jellied and congealed. At the climax of every brusquely constructed scene there is a shiver. A moment of truthfullness accessed by the artifice.
